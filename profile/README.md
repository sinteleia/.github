# 🚀 Sinteleia

*Engineering systems through collaboration, towards open ecosystems.*

---

## 🏢 Who we are

Sinteleia is an engineering company with over twenty years of experience in embedded systems, industrial electronics, and advanced device connectivity.

The name *Sinteleia* derives from the Greek concept of **working together towards a common goal** — a principle that has always guided our approach to technology and innovation.

Over the years, we have designed and delivered reliable, production-grade solutions ranging from low-level firmware to full system integration.

Our core expertise includes:
- Embedded systems and firmware development  
- Secure device connectivity and communication protocols  
- Industrial and medical-oriented electronic systems  

---

## 🧠 Our approach

We act as a **technology mediator**, transferring knowledge, methods, and solutions across different domains.

By working at the intersection of sectors, we:
- reuse proven technologies across contexts  
- reduce fragmentation between systems  
- enable interoperability between heterogeneous devices and platforms  

This cross-domain approach allows us to build solutions that are both **robust and adaptable**.

## 🌍 Towards open ecosystems

Today, we are evolving beyond project-specific development towards a more **open and collaborative model**.
